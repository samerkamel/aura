Epic 4: HR Utilities

Goal: To provide supplementary HR tools for asset management and compliant document generation.
Story 4.1: As an Admin, I want to manage a list of company assets (e.g., laptops, phones) and assign them to employees to track company property.
Story 4.2: As an Admin, I want to create and manage HR letter templates (e.g., for employment verification) with placeholders for employee data, supporting both English and Arabic.
Story 4.3: As an Admin, I want to manage a specific, legally compliant Arabic template for employee contracts, as required by Egyptian law.
Story 4.4: As an Admin, I want to generate a personalized document for an employee using any of the stored templates (including the official Arabic contract).
